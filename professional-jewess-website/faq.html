Questions

Good questions.

Is this a directory I can browse?

No. There is no public list of mentors to scroll. You tell us about yourself, and the team matches you thoughtfully — dignified and private by design.

How are matches made?

On what both sides share at signup: field, stage, community/observance (optional), goals, and availability. A person on the team confirms every match.

Is my information private?

Yes. Mentor details are private by default, and a mentor’s name is never public unless she chooses. Contact info is never shown to other members.

Do I have to commit long-term?

No. You can ask for a single one-time call, ongoing mentorship, or a small group — whatever fits your life this season.

Who can join as a mentor?

Frum women who’ve built a career and want to guide someone coming up behind them. You set your own capacity and visibility.

What does it cost?

This is a prototype; pricing isn’t set. The model is built to stay curated and trust-based, not a paid marketplace.
