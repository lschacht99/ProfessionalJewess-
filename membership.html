Membership model

A social platform, but quieter, curated, and professional.

The membership experience should not feel like an open feed. It should feel like a private room: approved profiles, thoughtful requests, and clear ways to connect.

For women seeking guidance

Members can find women by industry, city, experience, and specific questions: law school, first job, founding, leadership, medicine, design, finance, tech, education, and more.

Find mentorsAsk better questionsBuild confidence

For women ready to mentor

Mentors control their availability. They can offer one call a month, office hours, portfolio review, school advice, introductions, or limited guidance.

Platform flow.

This is the product architecture the full site should grow into.

1

Apply

A woman joins with her email, professional story, location, industry, and mentorship intent.

2

Approve

Profiles are reviewed before entering the member directory, keeping the network trusted and serious.

3

Connect

Members discover each other, request conversations, and schedule calls through the platform.

The live version should use real accounts.

For the next build, connect this prototype to Supabase, Firebase, Memberstack, or a custom backend. The first MVP can also connect forms to Google Sheets + Apps Script while membership is manually approved.
